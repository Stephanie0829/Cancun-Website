Within Cancun, there are various beaches with beautiful scenery and views. Resorts often include sandy banks where tourists can experience the waters, local restaurants, and take pictures with parrots.

Famous beaches include:

    Playa Delfines
    Puerto Morelos
    Playa Tortugas
    Playa Forum

Playa Delfines

This beach is not part of any resort or public lodgings, making it open with magnificent sights. The beach also includes foods and drinks from vendors, and a famous Cancun sign for picture-taking.

Puerto Morelos

Puerto Morelos is part of a fishing village, thus suitable for relaxation in a quiet location. It has a coral reef for snorkeling and visiting.

Playa Tortugas

This beach has famous clear waters and a ferry nearby. Like the other beaches, this location also has shopping, food, and drinks.

Playa Forum

Playa Forum is located next to various hotels and resorts, leading to it having multiple restaurants, refreshments, and entertainment. This location is more busy, with many people residing next to the beach.
