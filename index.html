Cancun is located within Mexico on the Yucatan peninsula. The land is divided between a downtown city area and tourist location. The destination includes both rich history and popular vacation destinations with its resorts and local lodgings.
The optimal time for visiting Cancun is January, with average temperatures from 23 to 27 degrees celsius, and the location is often used for touring during the Spring and Summer.

History of Cancun

    Mayan civilizations used to inhabit these lands, however, they left the peninsula around the 1400s
    The region was originally named Kan Kun, that stands for a cave of golden mythological sea creatures, snakes, and abundant flat land; this turned into Can Cun after Spanish influence
    1968, the Mexican government started to reorganize Cancun into a tourist location, creating connections to mainlands
